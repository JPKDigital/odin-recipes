Christmas Salad

A Holiday Tradition! The Perfect Salad

Nothing is more comforting than a beautiful, tasty salad on Christmas morning. This dish is the essence of balance while satiating the most intense appetites.

The classic combinations it contains will take you straigh to the north pole.

Ingredients:

  • Romaine Lettuce - washed and drained
  • Arugula - washed and drained
  • 4oz grilled chicken - sliced thin
  • 1/2 cup dried cranberries
  • 1/2 red pepper, diced
  • 1/2 cup sesame sticks
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese crumbles
  • 1/4 cup red onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup homemade Honey Mustard dressing

Steps:

  1. Combine the Romaine & Arugula in a bowl and mix well
  2. Lay out the following in pretty strips:
    • Grilled Chicken
    • Red Onion
    • Red Pepper
    • Sesame Sticks
    • Feta Cheese
    • Cranberries
  3. Serve with dressing on the side and enjoy!
  4. Enjoy!
