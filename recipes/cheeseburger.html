Classic Cheeseburger

The True American Class Cheeseburger

What could possibly be more American than a cheeseburger?

This may truly be the most juicy and flavorful meat that has ever come across your plate. Lets find out!

Ingredients:

  • 1lb ground beef - 80/20 or 85/15 fat percentage
  • Salt
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Garlic poweder
  • 1 Tomato, sliced
  • Sliced cheddar cheese
  • Kimmelweck Bun

Steps:

  1. Using a food scale, divinde the beef into 1/3rd lb patties
  2. Season both sides of each patty with salt,pepper, and garlic powder
  3. Pre-heat your grill and setup coals for direct cooking.
  4. Sear the burgers over high-heat on the grill until a dark crust is formed - about 4 minutes. Flip and sear the other side.
  5. Move to indirect heat. Place sliced cheddar on each burger and close the grill cover for 5 minutes
  6. Remove and rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  7. Enjoy!
