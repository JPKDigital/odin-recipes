Chicken & 40 Cloves

A Delicious dark-meat chicken & garlic dish!

This wonderful dish is incredibly easy to make while retaining the flavor profile of a 5-star restaurant plate!

By utilizing the juciest dark meat chicken with the bold garlic and slow cooking, you will produce some of the best meat you've ever had the pleasure of putting in your mouth!

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs Chicken Leg quarters (about 4 leg/drumstick combos)
  • 40 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 4 springs Thyme
  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)

Steps:

  1. Pre-heat a 12" cast-iron pan to medium-high heat and coat with a neutral oil (Avocado or Canola would be best).
  2. Season both sides of your chicken with salt & pepper.
  3. Place seasoned leg-quarters in pan, skin-side down. Allow to sear for 5 minutes or until a delicious golden crust has formed.
  4. Flip leg quarters meat-side down and add 1/2 cup olive oil & garlic cloves.
  5. Cover with foil and bake at 350* for 90 minutes or until chicken his an internal temperature of 180*.
  6. Remove and rest for 10 minutes before serving.
  7. Enjoy!
